Freya Crumble

Ingredients

  • 15 Apples
  • 2 Handfuls of flour
  • Fistful of sugar
  • Splash of milk
  • One lock from a fair Maiden's head

Instructions

  1. Perform all the usual steps to create an apple crumble
  2. After removing your crumble from the oven, take it outside and sever the Maiden's lock fresh from a fair Maiden's head
  3. Return indoors and return 1 hour later to find a still piping hot crumble, transformed by Freya into Freya Crumble
  4. Enjoy!
