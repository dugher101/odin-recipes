Loki Stew

Ingredients

  • Braising Beef
  • Beef Stock
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Spirit of mischief

Instructions

  1. First prepare your spirit of mischief by pulling a prank on an unsuspecting family member
  2. Once in the mischievious mood, make a beef stew as normal
  3. When dishing up the stew, laugh manically
  4. Enjoy!
