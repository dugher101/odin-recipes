Valhalla Pie

Ingredients

  • Shortcrust pastry
  • Chicken
  • Leek
  • Chicken stock
  • A knife used in battle

Instructions

  1. First prepare your chicken and leek pie as normal
  2. Once ready to serve cut into the pie using your battle-worn knife
  3. Feel the spirit of the gods infuse your pie, as your ancestor's in Valhalla look upon you
  4. Enjoy!
